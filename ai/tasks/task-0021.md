## Objective

Lock in the Phase-1 correctness properties from `design.md` ¬ß9 as permanent
regression gates, and ship the end-to-end CLI walk that exercises the full
revision-first path through real fixtures. After this PR, any future regression
in trigger-key stability, revision-key uniqueness, exec-key monotonicity,
statestore atomicity, or the revision-first CLI walk is caught by `go test`.
